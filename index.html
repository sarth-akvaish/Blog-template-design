The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) is the governing body for cricket in India. Its headquarters is situated at Wankhede stadium in Mumbai, Maharashtra. It is an autonomous organisation and does not come under National Sports Federation Of India.The board was formed in December 1928 as a society, registered under the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act. BCCI is an autonomous body and does not review any grants or fundings from Sports ministry of India.
